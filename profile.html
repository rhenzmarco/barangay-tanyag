Barangay Profile

LOCATION

Barangay Tanyag is located at the Municipality of City of Taguig, Island of Luzon. Barangay Tanyag is located near East Service Road on the North, the Barangay Sitio De Asis of Paranaque and Dela Rosa commercial of North Daanghari; NW Barangy Bagumbayan; S is the Barangay South Daanghari. Year 2009 the Barangay Bagong Tanyag is divided into three (3) Barangays; The mother Barangay - Barangay Tanyag, newly created Barangay - Barangay North Daanghari, Barangay South Daanghari
